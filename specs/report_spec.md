# Finiquito Report Specification

## Report sections

1. Cover / matter metadata.
2. Executive summary.
3. Legal classification.
4. Scenario comparison.
5. Calculation detail.
6. Tax and previsional treatment.
7. Employer cash cost.
8. Litigation risk flags.
9. Evidence checklist.
10. Recommended next steps.
11. Assumptions and rule version.
12. Appendix: calculation trace.

## Scenario comparison table

| Scenario | Net payable | Employer cost | Key assumptions | Risk |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|
| Employer intended case | calculated | calculated | employer-selected facts | base risk |
| Employee challenge case | calculated | calculated | employee claims improcedencia/nulidad/etc. | medium/high |
| Worst credible judicial case | calculated | calculated | plausible adverse ruling | high |

## Report flags

Report must display:

- `EXECUTABLE`
- `EXECUTABLE_WITH_WARNINGS`
- `NOT_EXECUTABLE_BLOCKED`
- `LEGAL_REVIEW_REQUIRED`

## Client disclaimer

The report must state that it is an advisory calculation based on provided documents and rule version, subject to lawyer review and factual/legal changes.
